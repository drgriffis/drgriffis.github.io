Brief Bio

I am a PhD student (2014-???) at Ohio State, in the department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE). I'm co-advised by Dr. Eric Fosler-Lussier (CSE) and Dr. Albert Lai (Department of Biomedical Informatics).

I'm currently working as a Pre-Doctoral Fellow with the Epidemiology and Biostatistics section of the National Institutes of Health Clinical Center, looking at how we can use NLP techniques to improve the workflow of the Social Security Administration's disability determination process.
